    Updated on September 18, 2025

    "The Hurt Inside: It's Me, Not You" is an EP containing three songs about personal insecurities and my anxieties, intended for The Drama Within, but left off the original release.

    The Drama Within (Turquoise Troubles) featuring Holding Onto You (Commentary)

    Published on April 5, 2025 by Lachie

    holding onto you started as literally two sentences from a random blurb of thoughts i had written down two years about not wanting to leave a toxic friendship.
    "when all you do is stir shit and lie for the hell of it, why am i still holding onto our friendship. why do i still hold onto you?"

    upon the revisiting of the feelings through writing this album, i returned to these two lines, and got inspired to write something about the feeling.
    and i love the antithesis between the original track and my track, the idea of someone running away when things get serious, and in my version, staying even when things turn for the worst.

    i feel like this is more a personal insecurity track, which is why i left this and a few other tracks i had written off 'the drama within'. but there's a certain affinity i feel towards this song that i needed to share this song.
    the hurt inside having to leave is the only that kept me there, but sometimes the staying is worse than the leaving.
    i promise at some point the songs i have left behind will be yours to hear (i have about an album's worth of unreleased songs that i want to eventually share from this era). but for now, i hope you enjoy this one.

    The Drama Within is Out Now!

    Published on March 16, 2025 by Lachie

    I can't believe it, my fifth ola album "The Drama Within" is out now!!!

    Such an exciting release for me, this has been months in the making and I'm so excited to be sharing it with you today!

    The creation process has been insane for album, I have written over 30 songs for this! Only 10 songs made the cut, and this time even added my intro, as a spoken word at the start.

    This album delves into a complicated history of my high school years, and as you guessed it, the drama that ensued during those years. This isn't only just about one person. Hell no, several people are the subjects of the stories that I detail throughout this album. You never know what people are capable of doing until they do it to you.

    I hope you enjoy it as much as I did making it, but now this chapter of my life is closed, and is now yours. Lots of love, Lachie
